The Idea

The idea of Hidden Gems is something like a mashup of Foursquare (for us old folks who remember those days when people used to 'check in' to places' and Instagram / Yelp. Hidden Gems aren't necessarily businesses - but more the places and spaces that create a community - that only the locals or the people who really spend tim there understand.

Hidden Gems might be temporary - like a Saturday morning football tailgating spot that only exists a few times a year during football season. But during that time - wouldn't it be great to have a site that could help you learn about the best spots to park and barbeque?
